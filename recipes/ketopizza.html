Keto Pizza

This keto pizza recipe is a great way to satisfy cravings and enjoy a delicious pizza without having to resort to cheat days. The low-carb ingredients used in the recipe, such as almond flour and coconut flour, provide a healthier alternative to traditional high-carb pizza crusts. Additionally, the use of natural and whole food ingredients, like mozzarella cheese and fresh vegetables, ensures that the pizza is packed with essential nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • tbsp psyllium husk powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 egg
  • Your preffered Sweetener
  • 1/4 cup low-carb pizza sauce
  • 1/4 lb minced beef
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400Â°F.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the mozzarella cheese for 1 minute.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the almond flour, coconut flour, psyllium husk powder, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the dry mixture to the melted mozzarella cheese and mix until combined.
  5. Add the olive oil and egg to the mixture
  6. Mix your sweetener with water to taste then add it to the mixture while stiring until a dough forms.
  7. Roll the dough out into a circle shape on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake the dough for 10-12 minutes, or until it is slightly golden.
  9. Spread the low-carb pizza sauce over the cooked crust.
  10. Add the minced beef, bell peppers, and black olives on top of the pizza.
  11. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese over the toppings.
  12. Bake the pizza for an additional 5-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  13. Slice and serve hot.
