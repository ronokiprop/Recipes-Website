The Ultimate Smoothie

The perfect avocado, peanut and chocolate milkshake for a ketogenic diet. This recipe meets the RDI for most nutrients although it might carry a little more saturated fat than desired. With 1900kcal, it's also convenient for someone who's trying to implement an OMAD diet.

Ingredients

  • 500ml Low Fat Milk
  • 2 Large Avocadoes
  • 200g of roasted peanuts
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • Natural Sweetener of your choice
  • Water and Ice Cubes

Steps

  1. Add the milk and peanuts to a blender
  2. Blend to a smooth consistency
  3. Chop the Avocadoes into the mixture
  4. Add 6 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  5. Add water in one cup increments and blend until the desired consistency is achieved
  6. Add the Sweetener in 2 tablespoon increments and blend until your desired taste is achieved
